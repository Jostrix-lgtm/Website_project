ORDERS



You can check the status of any order at any time simply by signing in to your Lozxy Member profile and visiting your orders page.



You can't make changes, but you can cancel an order within 30 minutes of placing it.

To cancel an order, first go to your orders page. If you're not signed in, you'll need to enter the order number from your confirmation email, along with your email address. Next, open the order you want to cancel, then tap or click the "Remove" button and follow the prompts.
