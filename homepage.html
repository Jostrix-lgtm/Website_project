About Us




Lozxy is a fashion store founded on 6 February 2022 with its main headquarters in Malaysia and branches located all over Asia. It is a rising business that has attract many customers due to its variety of products regarding fashion. Lozxy recently started an online store where it can further advance towards its mission. We plan to bring convenience to busy customers by helping them save time browsing for clothes and delivering it to them as fast as possible.
